The Monty Hall Problem

The late Monty Hall hosted several gameshows throughout his long career, most notably Let's Make a deal. In one game, he presented contestants with three doors. One of them hid a car while the other two had goats behind them. Part way through the game, Hall would reveal some information and offer the contestant a chance to change their guess. Let's see how it works.
